Robotics Overview

What is Robotics?

Robotics is a branch of technology that deals with the design, construction, operation, and application of robots. Robots are programmable machines that can carry out a series of actions autonomously or semi-autonomously. They are often used to perform tasks that are dangerous, repetitive, or require precision.

Robots are organized by their function, mobility, or structure. Functions include industrial, medical, domestic, and service robots. Mobility is either stationary or fixed and the structure of a robot can either be articulated, Cartesian, or SCARA armed. Some other types of robots to be aware of are humanoid, collaborative (cobots), and autonomous mobile robots (AMRs).
